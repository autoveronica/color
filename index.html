The Approach

Beyond Fashion,

NPO Japan Personal Colour Association

Colour is more than a visual choice. It shapes how you feel. The right shades can brighten your complexion, bring clarity to your features, and create a sense of ease in how you present yourself.

Using the structured methodology of the NPO Japan Personal Colour Association, I offer a precise and objective approach to personal colour analysis. Together, we will identify a palette that works in harmony with your natural colouring, so you can dress with confidence, not guesswork.
